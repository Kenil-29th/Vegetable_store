# Supplier Dashboard Guide

## What's New?

Each supplier now has their own **separate, isolated dashboard** where they can only see and manage their own products.

## Key Features

### 1. Isolated Product Management
- You can only see products that you created
- Other suppliers cannot see or modify your products
- Each supplier's dashboard is completely separate

### 2. Product Operations
You can perform the following operations on your products:

#### Create Products
- Click the "Add Product" button in your dashboard
- Fill in product details (name, SKU, category, price, quantity, description)
- Upload product image (optional)
- Products are automatically assigned to your supplier account

#### View Your Products
- Your dashboard displays only your products in a table format
- See product details: name, SKU, category, price, quantity, and status
- View statistics: Total products and active products count

#### Edit Products
- Click the "Edit" button on any of your products
- Update any product information
- Changes are saved only for your products

#### Delete Products
- Click the "Delete" button to remove a product
- Confirm the deletion
- Only your products can be deleted by you

### 3. Dashboard Statistics
Your dashboard shows:
- **Total Products**: Count of all your products
- **Active Products**: Count of your products that are currently active

## Access Your Dashboard

1. **Login** with your supplier credentials
2. Navigate to `/supplier/dashboard`
3. Your dashboard will load showing only your products

## Security

- All operations require authentication
- You cannot access or modify other suppliers' products
- Admins can view and manage all products across all suppliers

## Product Visibility

### Your Dashboard (Supplier View)
- Shows only YOUR products
- Full edit/delete capabilities on your products

### Customer View
- Shows ALL products from ALL suppliers
- Customers can browse and purchase from any supplier

### Admin Dashboard
- Shows ALL products from ALL suppliers
- Full management capabilities across all suppliers

## Getting Started

1. Register as a supplier
2. Login to your account
3. Access your dashboard at `/supplier/dashboard`
4. Click "Add Product" to create your first product
5. Manage your products through the dashboard

If you need help or have questions about managing your products, please contact the admin team.
